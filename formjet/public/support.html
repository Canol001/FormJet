Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up a form with FormJet?

Create a form in your dashboard, copy the provided endpoint URL, and add it to your HTML form's `action` attribute with `method="POST"`. Refer to the Documentation for a detailed guide.

Why am I not receiving email notifications?

Check your spam folder and ensure the recipient email is correctly set in your form settings. If the issue persists, contact support below.

Can I integrate FormJet with other tools?

Yes! FormJet supports direct integrations with various tools. Check the Documentation for a list of supported integrations.

How do I access my form submissions?

Submissions are saved to your FormJet Inbox, accessible from your dashboard. You can view, analyze, and export them as needed.
