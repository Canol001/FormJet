FormJet lets you connect any HTML contact form to your email without writing any backend code. Just copy and paste the form below, and you're good to go!

Step 1: Copy This Form

Paste this form in your HTML and replace YOUR_USER_ID with your actual FormJet ID.

<form action="https://your-formjet-domain.com/send/YOUR_USER_ID" method="POST">
  <input type="text" name="name" placeholder="Your Name" required />
  <input type="email" name="email" placeholder="Your Email" required />
  <textarea name="message" placeholder="Your Message" required></textarea>
  <button type="submit">Send Message</button>
</form>

Step 2: Where Messages Go

When a visitor submits your form, the message is securely emailed to the address linked to your FormJet user ID.

Step 3: Tips for Success

  • Use a real FormJet user ID in the URL (e.g. /send/12345).
  • Host your site with HTTPS for secure form submission.
  • Ensure the form uses POST method.
  • You don't need JavaScript or server-side code ‚Äî just HTML!
